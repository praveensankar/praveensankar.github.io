I am a PhD Research Fellow working on Blockchain and Privacy in the ND research Group at the University of Oslo, Norway. I have research experience in Blockchain, Named Data Networking and Lattice Cryptography. My research interests are in Blockchain, Distributed Computing, Decentralized Identity and Applied Cryptography
